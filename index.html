💡 Why People Use Perfember

Whatever your reason,
we've got you covered.

Performance tracking means different things to different people. Perfember works for all of them.

💰

Going for a pay rise?

Build an undeniable case. Log every win, every piece of positive feedback, every target hit — so when the conversation comes, you walk in with hard evidence, not vague memories.

🚀

Gunning for a promotion?

Show your growth over time, not just a snapshot. A running record of your contributions, skills developed, and problems solved makes a far more compelling promotion case than a rushed summary.

⚠️

Managing a difficult employee?

Document issues accurately and fairly as they happen, not weeks later from memory. A clear, timestamped record protects you and gives the employee a fair chance to improve.

🆕

Starting a new job or role?

The first 90 days matter most. Log your early wins, the problems you solve, and the relationships you build. When your probation review comes, you'll have a ready-made case for why you're exactly what they hired.

💼

Changing jobs and need a strong CV?

Your logs become your career portfolio. Concrete achievements, real metrics, and outcomes you can be proud of — ready to pull into any application, LinkedIn update, or interview at a moment's notice.

🎯

Tracking progress against goals?

Set goals once and log against them as you go. See clearly who is on track and who isn't — and generate reports that show real progress, not guesswork.

🏢 For Senior Leaders
📊

Building a case for headcount?

When budget season comes, gut feel isn't enough. A documented record of team output, capacity constraints, and delivery across quarters gives you the hard evidence to justify your next hire — before anyone challenges it.

🏛️

Reporting up to the board or exec team?

Turn months of team activity into a polished executive summary in minutes. Know exactly what your team delivered, where they excelled, and where investment is needed — so you walk into every senior meeting fully prepared.
