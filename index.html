💡 Why People Use Perfember

we've got you covered.

Performance tracking means different things to different people. Perfember works for all of them.

💰

Going for a pay rise?

Build an undeniable case. Log every win, every piece of positive feedback, every target hit — so when the conversation comes, you walk in with hard evidence, not vague memories.

🚀

Gunning for a promotion?

Show your growth over time, not just a snapshot. A running record of your contributions, skills developed, and problems solved makes a far more compelling promotion case than a rushed summary.

⚠️

Managing a difficult employee?

Document issues accurately and fairly as they happen, not weeks later from memory. A clear, timestamped record protects you and gives the employee a fair chance to improve.

🙌

Got someone too modest to shout about their work?

Some of your best people will never talk themselves up. Log their achievements for them and make sure their good work doesn't go unnoticed when it matters most.

📋

Just want to stay on top of things?

No big agenda — just better habits. A quick daily or weekly log means you always know where you and your team stand. No more scrambling before reviews.

🎯

Tracking progress against goals?

Set goals once and log against them as you go. See clearly who is on track and who isn't — and generate reports that show real progress, not guesswork.
